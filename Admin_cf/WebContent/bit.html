Here’s What You Need to Know About BIT

The Bachelor of Information Technology is an academic program of Purbanchal University comprising of core unit and advanced unit. The core unit of Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT) covers the fundamentals of information technology that provide students with knowledge and skills in programming, systems design, computer networks and communication. The advanced unit enables students to exercise, develop and apply their knowledge and skills in the areas of Multimedia, Artificial Intelligence, Mobile communication, Internet and World Wide Web. BIT is a four-year (eight semesters), 140 credit hours academic program which gives knowledge and expertise needed for a long-term career in IT rather than the basic skills to get the first job. The course offers scientific and technical depth along with study in areas such as management, communications and aspects of human behavior, all of which are vital to the success of an IT professional.


Eligibility:


  • The candidate must have completed intermediate/ certificate level or 10+2 or equivalent degree from any recognized university/ board
  • Candidate should have appeared atleast one paper Mathematics in PCL or +2 Level
  • Candidate must have scored 45% marks
  • Candidate are required to appear in the entrance examination test conducted by Purbanchal University
  • Only short listed candidates from written test will be interviewed and selected for admission

Career Options:


Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT) Graduates are well equipped with technical skills and are highly sought after by Information and Communication industry. They have excellent opportunities available for progressing into management and leadership positions.
BIT graduates may opt for positions in IT-related fields like Network Management, Internet & Intranet Management, Database Administration, Web Administration, Advertising, Information Management, Application Development, Network Consultancy, Distributed Applications Development and Multimedia Technology.
