Here’s What You Need to Know About BSC.CSIT

BSc.CSIT is a 4 years degree affiliated to Tribhuvan University. It consists of 8 semesters. By the name of the degree it is clear to us that this course is a blend of Computer Science (CS) and Information Technology (IT). Although Computer Science and Information Technology graduates can go for similar jobs. However, Computer Science course is focused towards the application development whereas Information Technology is focused towards setting up network infrastructures, and database along with computer systems. Hence, BSc. CSIT focus towards both CS and IT. We provide you information of various colleges offering Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information technology. It is a big step for students who want to develop career in Information Technology. Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information technology course gives theoretical and practical knowledge which enables student participate effectively in solving problems related with IT. By giving you the names of Colleges facilitating the Bsc. CSIT program you can choose according to your preference and proximity for studying this course. We give you teaching pedagogy of different academic institutes and their evaluation system to give understanding of what one can expect from their college after they have joined.


Eligibility


Candidates applying for Bsc. CSIT program you need:


  • To complete 12 years of education in science stream from any board recognized by Tribhuvan University.
  • Pass 12 in minimum of second division score.
  • Should have successfully passed the entrance examination conducted by TU securing at least 35% marks.
  • Compiled with all the application procedures.
  • There are more than 50 colleges all over Nepal which offer the B.Sc. CSIT course.
  • Fee structure in government based constituent campus is around 2 Lakhs and Private Colleges fees ranges from NPR 3 Lakhs to NPR 9 Lakhs.
  • There are also scholarships available, so it is likely the fees could be less.

Who should study BSc. CSIT?


Students who wants to work either in a software development company or who wants to work in the field of network should go for BSc. CSIT. Some of the careers for BSc. CSIT graduates is mentioned below:


  • System Analyst
  • Project Manager
  • Software Developer
  • Web Developer
  • Mobile Application Developer
  • Quality Assurance Expert
  • IT Manager
  • Web Designer
  • Graphics Designer
  • Animation Expert
  • Interface Designer
  • Network Administrator
  • System Administrator
  • Network Security Expert
  • Database Developer
  • Database Administrator and many more.
